## What to Look for in an Electric Height Adjustable Desk Frame

### Weight Capacity Matters More Than You Think

Don't underestimate your equipment weight. A 60-inch desktop alone weighs 40-50 pounds. Add monitors, computers, books, and accessories, and you're easily approaching 100+ pounds. Choose a frame rated for at least 50% more than your calculated load.

### Motor Quality Determines Longevity

Single-motor frames struggle with heavier loads and wear out faster. Dual-motor systems distribute weight evenly and provide redundancy if one motor fails. Look for motors rated for 10,000+ cycles—roughly five years of daily use.

### Height Range Must Match Your Needs

Standard frames adjust from 22-48 inches. Taller users (over 6'2") need frames reaching 48+ inches for comfortable standing. Shorter users benefit from lower minimum heights for proper sitting ergonomics.

### Stability at Standing Height

Frame wobble becomes pronounced at standing height, especially with multiple monitors. Look for frames with crossbeam supports and heavy-gauge steel construction. Reviews mentioning "rock solid at standing height" indicate good stability.

### Control Features Worth Paying For

Memory presets eliminate guesswork when switching between sitting and standing positions. Anti-collision protection prevents damage when objects block the desk path. These features add cost but provide significant convenience and safety benefits.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How much weight can electric standing desk frames handle?

Most quality electric frames support 220-350 pounds. Budget models handle 150-220 pounds, while commercial frames exceed 400 pounds. Consider your desktop weight plus all equipment when choosing capacity.

### Do electric standing desk frames wobble?

Some wobble is normal, especially at maximum height. Higher-end frames minimize wobble through reinforced construction and quality motors. Frames under $200 typically show noticeable wobble at standing height.

### How long do electric standing desk motors last?

Quality dual-motor systems last 5-7 years with daily use. Single motors wear out faster, typically lasting 2-3 years. Motor quality correlates strongly with frame price—budget motors fail sooner.

### Can I use any desktop with electric standing desk frames?

Most frames accommodate standard rectangular desktops 48-80 inches wide. Check mounting hole compatibility and weight limits. Some frames include desktop mounting hardware; others require separate purchase.

### Are electric standing desks noisy?

Modern electric frames operate at 45-55 decibels—quieter than normal conversation. Budget frames may be louder due to cheaper motors. Look for specifications mentioning "whisper quiet" operation.
