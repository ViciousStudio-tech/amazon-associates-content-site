## Buying Guide: Choosing Your Perfect Sleep Machine

### Sound Type Considerations

**Mechanical vs. Digital**: Mechanical machines like the Dohm create authentic sounds but offer less variety. Digital machines provide multiple options but may sound less natural.

**Sound Variety**: Consider whether you want simple white noise or multiple soundscape options. I find 3-6 quality sounds better than dozens of mediocre ones.

### Essential Features to Consider

**Volume Range**: Look for machines that can go truly quiet for light sleepers or loud enough to mask significant noise disturbances.

**Power Options**: Battery operation adds convenience but may limit volume. AC power provides consistent performance.

**Timer Functions**: Helpful if you only need noise to fall asleep, saving energy and potentially extending device life.

**Size and Portability**: Consider where you'll use it. Bedroom-only machines can be larger, but travel requires compact design.

### Smart Features Worth Considering

Modern machines increasingly offer app connectivity, sunrise simulation, and integration with smart home systems. While not essential, these features can significantly enhance your sleep routine if you're willing to invest in the technology.
