## Buying Guide: What to Look For in Subscription-Free Doorbell Cameras

### Storage Options

The most critical factor is how you'll store recordings. Look for doorbells with built-in storage, microSD card slots, or genuine free cloud storage. Avoid models that only offer short "trial periods" before requiring paid subscriptions.

### Video Quality and Features

Don't sacrifice essential features to avoid subscriptions. Look for at least 1080p resolution, night vision, and two-way audio. Many subscription-free models now offer 2K video and AI-powered detection that rivals premium paid services.

### Installation Requirements

Consider whether you want battery-powered or hardwired installation. Battery models offer more flexibility but require regular charging. Hardwired options provide consistent power but may need professional installation if you lack existing doorbell wiring.

### Smart Home Integration

Check compatibility with your existing smart home ecosystem. Some doorbell cameras work better with Alexa, while others integrate seamlessly with Google Home or Apple HomeKit. This becomes especially important when you're not relying on the manufacturer's cloud service.
