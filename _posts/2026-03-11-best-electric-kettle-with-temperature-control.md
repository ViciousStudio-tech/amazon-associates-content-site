## Buying Guide: What to Look for in Temperature-Controlled Electric Kettles

### 1. Temperature Range and Accuracy

Look for kettles offering temperatures from at least 160°F to 212°F. This range covers everything from delicate green teas (160-175°F) to black teas and coffee (195-212°F). Check reviews for accuracy – the best models maintain temperatures within 3-5 degrees of the target.

### 2. Capacity and Size

Consider your household's needs. Single users might prefer compact 0.8-1 liter models, while families should look for 1.5-1.7 liter capacity. Remember that larger kettles take longer to heat and require more counter space.

### 3. Control Interface

Decide between preset temperatures or variable control. Presets are simpler and often more accurate, while variable control offers flexibility. Digital displays provide precision, while dial controls offer intuitive operation.

### 4. Build Quality and Features

Stainless steel construction lasts longer and doesn't affect taste, though it costs more than plastic. Look for features like keep-warm functions (essential for multiple servings), auto shut-off (safety), and cord storage (organization).
