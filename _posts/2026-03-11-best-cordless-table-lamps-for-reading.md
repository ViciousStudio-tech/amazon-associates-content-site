## What to Look for in the Best Cordless Table Lamps for Reading

### Battery Life and Charging

Battery performance varies dramatically across models. Look for lamps offering at least 6-8 hours of continuous use at your preferred brightness level. I've found that manufacturers often overstate battery life, so expect real-world performance to be 10-20% lower than advertised.

USB-C charging has become the standard, offering faster charging times than older USB-A ports. Some models include wireless charging bases, though these typically cost more and aren't necessary for most users.

### Light Quality and Adjustability

The best cordless reading lamps offer multiple brightness levels and, ideally, adjustable color temperature. Cool white light (5000K-6500K) provides excellent clarity for detailed reading, while warm white (2700K-3000K) creates a more relaxing atmosphere for leisure reading.

Look for CRI (Color Rendering Index) ratings above 80, with 90+ being ideal. Higher CRI values mean colors appear more natural and text contrast improves, reducing eye strain.

### Positioning and Stability

Adjustable arms or necks let you direct light exactly where needed without moving the entire lamp. However, ensure the base provides adequate stability—lightweight lamps with long adjustable arms can tip easily.

Consider how you'll primarily use the lamp. Bedside readers need different positioning capabilities than desk workers or armchair readers.

### Design and Build Quality

While aesthetics are personal, build quality directly impacts longevity. Metal construction generally outlasts plastic, though it increases weight and cost. Touch controls feel more modern than physical buttons but can be accidentally triggered.

Consider your existing decor when choosing between contemporary LED designs and traditional lamp styles with fabric or glass shades.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How long do cordless reading lamp batteries typically last?

Most quality cordless reading lamps provide 6-12 hours of continuous use, depending on brightness settings. LED models generally offer longer battery life than other technologies. Expect battery capacity to decrease gradually over 2-3 years of regular use.

### Can cordless table lamps provide enough light for reading?

Yes, modern cordless LED lamps can easily provide sufficient illumination for comfortable reading. Look for models with at least 400-600 lumens output. The key is proper positioning—focused light directly on your reading material is more effective than general room lighting.

### Are cordless reading lamps safe to leave charging overnight?

Quality cordless lamps include built-in charging protection that prevents overcharging. However, I recommend unplugging chargers once batteries reach full capacity to maximize battery lifespan. Most modern lamps indicate charging status with LED indicators.

### What's the difference between cordless table lamps and regular battery-powered book lights?

Cordless table lamps typically offer higher light output, longer battery life, and more stable positioning than small clip-on book lights. Table lamps also provide broader light coverage, making them better for reading larger books or documents.

### How do I maintain my cordless reading lamp's battery?

Avoid completely draining the battery before recharging, and don't leave the lamp plugged in continuously after reaching full charge. Store the lamp with a partial charge (around 50%) if not using it for extended periods.
