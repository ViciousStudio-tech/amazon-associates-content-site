  <p style="font-size:17px;color:var(--text-secondary);margin-bottom:32px;line-height:1.7;">DwellPicks exists because finding the right product for your home shouldn't take hours of research. We do the deep dive so you don't have to.</p>

  <h2 style="font-family:var(--font-serif);font-size:22px;color:var(--navy);margin-bottom:12px;">Our Mission</h2>
  <p>Every guide on DwellPicks is written to answer one question: <em>what is actually the best product for this specific use case?</em> Not just the most popular. Not just the most expensive. The one that solves the problem best.</p>
  <p>We focus on home decor, smart home, kitchen, lighting, and organization — the categories where a single good recommendation can meaningfully improve your day-to-day life.</p>

  <h2 style="font-family:var(--font-serif);font-size:22px;color:var(--navy);margin-top:32px;margin-bottom:12px;">Our Process</h2>
  <p>Before we publish a guide, we analyze hundreds of customer reviews, expert opinions, and product specifications. We look for patterns — what do people love, what breaks, what surprises them after 6 months of use. Our recommendations reflect that research.</p>
